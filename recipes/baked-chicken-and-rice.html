Ingredients

  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 can milk (use the mushroom soup can)
  • 3/4 cup uncooked regular rice
  • 1 can (4 oz) mushroom stems
  • 1 packet dry onion soup mix (divided into two parts)
  • 6 pieces of chicken (on bone)

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350° F.
  2. Mix together cream of mushroom soup and milk. Reserve Â½ cup of the mixture.
  3. Add rice, mushrooms, and 1/2 packet dry onion soup mix to mixture. Pour into ungreased baking dish (11x7).
  4. Arrange chicken pieces on rice mixture. Pour reserved soup mixture over chicken and sprinkle with remaining onion soup packet. Cover and bake for 1 hour. Uncover and bake for 15 mins.
