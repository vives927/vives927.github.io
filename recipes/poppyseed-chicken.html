--- layout: recipe title: Poppyseed Chicken category: Main Dish published: 11/05/2014 servings: 4-6 source: Ashley Hoard ---

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts, cut up and cooked
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 8-oz tub of sour cream
  • 1 stick of butter, melted
  • 1 roll Ritz crackers, crushed
  • Poppyseeds

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350° F. Coat baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Place chicken breasts in baking dish. Combine cream of chicken soup and sour cream and pour over the chicken.
  3. Mix Ritz crackers and butter, and sprinkle on top of chicken mixture. Sprinkle poppyseeds on top of that.
  4. Bake for 35 mins. Serve over rice or noodles.
