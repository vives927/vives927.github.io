Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 sirloin steak, cut into 1-inch chunks
  • 1 envelope Lipton® Recipe Secrets® Onion Mushroom Soup Mix
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 can (14.5-oz) new potatoes, drained and cut into chunks
  • 1 bag (10-oz) frozen peas and carrots

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in 12-inch nonstick skillet over medium-high heat and brown steak.
  2. Stir in remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil over high heat.
  3. Reduce heat to low and simmer uncovered 10 minutes or until steak is tender.

My Comments:

I couldn't bring myself to use canned potatoes, so my recipe took a little bit longer.
