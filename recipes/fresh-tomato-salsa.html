--- layout: recipe title: Fresh Tomato Salsa category: Appetizer published: 11/05/2014 servings: 16 source: Genevieve Nelson ---

Ingredients

  • 8-10 large tomatoes
  • 1 bunch of cilantro, chopped
  • 2 medium onions, chopped
  • 6 hot peppers, chopped
  • a few cloves of garlic, chopped
  • salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • lime juice to taste

Instructions

  1. Drop tomatoes into boiling water until the skins come off easily. Then, remove skins and chop.
  2. Throw everything in the blender, and you're done!

My Comments:

For the hot peppers, I recommend wearing gloves.
