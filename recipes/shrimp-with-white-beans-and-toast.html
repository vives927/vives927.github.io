Ingredients

  • 4 slices country bread
  • 6 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1 lb frozen peeled and deveined medium shrimp, thawed
  • kosher salt and black pepper
  • 1 15-oz can white beans, rinsed
  • 4 cups baby arugula (about 3 oz)

Instructions

  1. Spread the bread on both sides with 2 tbsp of the butter.
  2. Cook the bread in a large skillet over medium heat until browned, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Remove from the skillet.
  3. Wipe out the skillet and melt the remaining 4 tbsp of butter over medium-high heat. Add the garlic and cook, stirring, for 1 minute.
  4. Add the wine and bring to a boil.
  5. Add the shrimp, season with 1/2 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp pepper, and simmer until opaque throughout, 2 to 3 minutes.
  6. Fold in the beans and arugula and cook just until the beans are heated through, 1 to 2 minutes. Serve over the bread.
