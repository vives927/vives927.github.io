--- layout: recipe title: Red-Skin Potato Salad category: Side Dish published: 11/08/2014 servings: 1 9-inch pie source: Ian Nelson ---

Ingredients

  • 7 red-skin potatoes, cut into bite-sized pieces and boiled
  • 4 eggs, boiled and chopped
  • 8 strips of bacon, cooked and chopped
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 stalks of celery, finely chopped
  • 1 cup mayo
  • 1/4 cup mustard
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together and refrigerate.
