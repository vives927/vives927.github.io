Creamy Cheese Pie

Back to Recipes
Category:  Dessert
Date Published: 11/05/2014
Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 9-in graham cracker crust
  • 1 8 oz block cream cheese (room temp.)
  • 1 14 oz can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 c lemon juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 tbsp sugar

Instructions

  1. Beat cream cheese with hand mixer for 3-5 minutes, until smooth.
  2. Add condensed milk slowly, while mixing.
  3. Mix in lemon juice and vanilla.
  4. Pour the cream mixture into the graham cracker crust and refrigerate for at least two hours.

Source: Teri Miles
