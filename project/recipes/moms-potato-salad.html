Mom's Potato Salad

Back to Recipes
Category:  Side Dish
Date Published: 11/05/2014
Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 4 big potatoes
  • 2-3 hard-boiled eggs
  • Pickles or relish
  • Celery
  • Bacon
  • Kenâ€™s Italian Dressing
  • Mayo
  • Salt & pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil potatoes whole until they are soft enough to eat. Chop into bite-sized pieces and place in a large bowl.
  2. Chop and add eggs, pickles, celery and bacon to the mixture.
  3. Add Italian dressing, mayo, salt and pepper to the mixture.

Source: Valerie Ferrin
