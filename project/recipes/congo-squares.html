Congo Squares (Blondies)

Back to Recipes
Category:  Dessert
Date Published: 11/05/2014
Servings: 12

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 1/4 cup shortening
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 3/4 cups flour (optional: substitute 3/4 cup oatmeal for 3/4 cup flour)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 10 oz chocolate chips (optional: substitute 3/4 cup white chocolate chips and 1/2 cup craisins)

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350° F.
  2. Cream together: margarine, shortening, brown sugar, white sugar.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla to the mixture.
  4. Add flour, baking powder, and salt to the mixture.
  5. Add chocolate chips to the mixture.
  6. Press mixture into greased 9x12 baking dish. Bake for 25-35 mins.

Source: Valerie Ferrin
