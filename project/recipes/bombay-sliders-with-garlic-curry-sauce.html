Bombay Sliders with Garlic Curry Sauce

Back to Recipes
Category:  Main Dish
Date Published: 11/04/2014
Servings: 4

Ingredients

For the sauce:

  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 1/4 tsp curry powder
  • 1 1/2 tbsp plain yogurt
  • 1 1/2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 garlic clove, minced

For the meat:

  • 2 lbs ground turkey
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise, divided
  • 4 tsp curry powder, divided
  • 6 tbsp chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup minced green onions
  • 2 tbsp minced peeled fresh ginger
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 3/4 tsp hot chili powder or Hungarian hot paprika
  • 1 tsp salt

Other:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 12 small dinner rolls, cut horizontally in half, lightly toasted

Instructions

  1. Combine sauce ingredients in a small bowl. Let stand at room temperature while preparing sliders.
  2. Combine meat ingredients in a large bowl. Mix with fork or hands just until blended (do not overmix). Divide mixture into 12 equal portions. Using wet hands, form each portion into patty about 1/2 inch thick.
  3. Preheat broiler or heat 1 tbsp oil in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. If broiling patties, brush rimmed baking sheet with 1 tbsp oil and arrange patties on sheet. Broil (or cover patties in skillet and cook in batches) until cooked through, pressing patties down lightly with spatula before turning over, about 3 minutes per side (or 4 minutes per side if cooking in skillet).
  4. Place patties on bottom halves of rolls. Top each patty with sauce. Cover with roll tops. Place 3 sliders on each of 4 plates and serve.
