Barley Vegetable Soup with Sausage

Back to Recipes
Category:  Main Dish
Date Published: 11/05/2014
Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound hot Italian turkey sausage
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 5 cups water
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes (with juice)
  • 3/4 cup Quaker quick barley
  • 1/2 cup sliced fennel
  • 1/2 cup sliced carrots
  • 4 beef boullion cubes
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil, crushed
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Dash of crushed red pepper
  • 1/2 cup frozen corn

Instructions

  1. In a 4-qt saucepan or Dutch oven, brown meat. Add onion and garlic; cook until onion is tender.
  2. Stir in the rest of the ingredients, except for the corn. Cover; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add frozen corn; cook about 10 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
