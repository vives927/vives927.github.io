Tuna Casserole

Back to Recipes
Category:  Main Dish
Date Published: 11/08/2014
Servings: 3-4

Ingredients

  • 8 oz egg noodles, cooked
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 10-oz package frozen peas and carrots
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 12 oz tuna, drained
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/3 cup breadcrumbs

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together (except for breadcrumbs), and pour into a baking dish.
  2. Sprinkle breadcrumbs on top. Bake at 350° F.

Source: Valerie Ferrin
